Özgür Aktan

Clinical Psychologist, Psychotherapist. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Psychology at Çanakkale Onsekiz Mart University, then continued my studies at Boğaziçi University. I obtained my Master’s degree in Clinical Psychology from Maltepe University.

My Master’s Thesis: “Patients Diagnosed with Depression: A Comparison of Defense Mechanisms Used by Undiagnosed Individuals”. The aim of the research was to explore similarities and differences in defense mechanisms for a better understanding of depression and to support mental health professionals.

Since 2012, I have been working as a clinical psychologist and active psychotherapist. I gained professional experience at NP İstanbul, Humanitas, Erickson Institute Istanbul, as well as in projects under the Turkish Psychological Association. I also hold an international coaching certification (ICF).

Professional experience includes: psychological counseling, coaching, coordinating psychological centers, supervision, and practice in psychiatric clinics and mental health centers.
